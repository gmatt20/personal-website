About Me


Welcome to MENTAL DEV TECH, where mental health meets technology. My name is Matthew, and I specialize in creating responsive, beautiful, and insightful websites that are tailored to the unique needs of mental health providers, organizations, and businesses.

With a deep understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I bring your visions to life with intuitive designs and user-friendly interfaces. My focus is on crafting UI experiences that not only look great but also resonate with users, providing them with the tools and information they need in a seamless and engaging way.





My Services


Mental Health Providers & Organizations:


My passion with mental health and web development creates a unique personal touch to making your websites beautiful and all-inclusive.

I understand that keeping up with a practice or organization is a whole job in it of itself. Designing, coding, and managing a website is a whole another job that many professionals and individuals simply do not have time for.

My goal is to help you alleviate this stress so that you can focus on your practice clients with a website that reflects your mission and values. Whether you need an informative platform for your practice or an interactive site for a larger organization, I design with empathy and precision.



For The Start Ups, General Businesses, and General Individuals:


From restaurants to non-profits, I create websites that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. My designs are responsive, ensuring they look and work great on any device, and are tailored to meet the specific needs of your business.



Why Choose Me?


  • Front-End Expertise: With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I build sites that are not only visually appealing but also performs superb and is easy to navigate.


  • Focus on Mental Health: I am passionate about mental health and committed to helping providers and organizations communicate their message effectively online.


  • Responsive Design: Every website I create is designed to be fully responsive, ensuring a great user experience on desktops, tablets, and smartphones.


  • Insightful Content and Beautifully Crafted Websites: You worked hard for your passion, and you deserve your website to be made beautifully! I aim to create websites that not only attract visitors but also engage them, offering insightful and relevant content.


While I currently specialize in front-end development, I'm continuously expanding my skills and knowledge. Back-end development is coming soon, allowing me to offer even more comprehensive solutions for your web needs.



Thank you for joining me on this journey, and I look forward to sharing my passion for mental health and technology with you.
