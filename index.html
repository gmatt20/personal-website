About Me


Hi! I’m Matthew, I am a software engineer with a passion for blending creativity and technology to build impactful solutions. With a strong foundation in front-end development in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I specialize in creating responsive websites and dynamic web applications that prioritize an exceptional user experience. My expertise extends to modern tools like Tailwind CSS and React, which I use to design sleek, maintainable, and high-performance web solutions.

I began my journey by mastering front-end fundamentals and am now expanding my skill set to full-stack development, diving deeper into back-end technologies like Node.js. Driven by continuous learning and innovation, I thrive on solving complex challenges with clean, maintainable code and a deep understanding of user needs.



With a Bachelor’s degree in Psychology and a minor in Computer Science, I bring a unique perspective to software development. My background enables me to understand user behavior deeply, apply UI/UX design principles, and foster strong collaboration through clear communication. This scientific mindset complements my engineering creativity, allowing me to deliver solutions that are both intuitive and impactful.



Whether I’m writing scalable code, refining a user interface, or solving a complex technical challenge, I always keep the user at the forefront of my decision-making. By combining an engineering mindset with a passion for innovation, I aim to create solutions that truly make a difference and bring inspiring ideas to life.





Why Choose Me?


  • Front-End Expertise: With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I build sites that are not only visually appealing but also performs superb and is easy to navigate.


  • Responsive Design: Every website I create is designed to be fully responsive, ensuring a great user experience on desktops, tablets, and smartphones.


  • Insightful Content and Beautifully Crafted Websites: I aim to create websites that not only attract visitors but also engage them, offering insightful and relevant content.


While I currently specialize in front-end development, I'm continuously expanding my skills and knowledge. Back-end development is coming soon, allowing me to offer even more comprehensive solutions for your web needs.
