About Me


Hello! I'm Matthew, and I have a for mental health. My journey into the world of psychology began with a deep curiosity about the human mind and a desire to help others navigate their mental and emotional well-being.

Through volunteering for the underrepresented communities in Riverside, I have found a satisfying and deep pleasure in releasing the burdens of suffering of the human condition through humor and optimism. This has lead me to pursue to be a clinical psychologist.





While pursuing my studies in psychology, I discovered the power of technology to communicate the importance of mental health. This realization led me to combine my love for programming with my commitment to mental health. I believe that digital tools and platforms can play a transformative role in making mental health resources more accessible, engaging, and effective.


Through my work, I aim to create applications that promote reflection, self-awareness, and emotional resilience. Whether it's through interactive mental health apps, insightful blogs, or purposeful websites, I strive to communicate the importance of mental health in our daily lives.


As I continue on my journey to becoming a psychologist, I am dedicated to using programming as a medium to support and uplift those who may be struggling. I believe that by merging technology with psychology, we can create innovative solutions that foster a healthier, more compassionate world.



Thank you for joining me on this journey, and I look forward to sharing my passion for mental health and technology with you.
